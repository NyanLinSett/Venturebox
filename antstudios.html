ANT Studios × Venturebox

Nyan Lin Sett
15. 12. 2022

Intro

“Improve your Learning. Widen your Knowledge” This is ANT Studio’s motto, and they’ve stood by it ever since they were founded in early 2022. ANT Studios is a flourishing start-up organization with a mission to reform education. The team has worked effortfully to make learning a fun, enjoyable, efficient and rewarding activity. And today, they’ve joined hands with Venturebox.



About ANT Studios

ANT studios was founded in March 2022 by a trio of ambitious and hardworking young men: Alvin, Noah and Theo. Their initials formed the name ANT. Alvin is a highly competent individual who is the engine that drove ANT Studios to its success. Fueled by immense intrinsic motivation, he work relentlessly and is the main man behind ANT Studios’ take-off. Noah played a key role as well. Despite the unfavorable situations he was put in, he didn’t allow constraints to hinder him from producing quality work. Lastly was Theo. Theo is the creative source of ANT Studios; he is a dreamer, and his spontaneous bursts of energy aided in consolidating creative breakthroughs.



Mission

ANT Studio's mission is to make education and learning more accessible, convenient and enjoyable. Regardless of your gender, race, or location, they will make it possible for you to acquire knowledge and teach you ways to process and utilize it. ANT studios collect information from various sources, curate it in one concrete and accessible location, which makes it easier for knowledge seekers and prevents the loss of unnecessary time and effort in search of learning material. They focus mainly on academic subjects, ranging from proficiency skills like English to and Sciences like Biology and Chemistry. They’ve also began to expand their domain to other skills like communications, information literacy and economics.



What’s the collaboration about?

The purpose of the collaboration seems apparent and obvious: to provide better educational access. Venturebox has worked with many startup founders as well as enterprise leaders, and we'd noticed a common cause of struggle for those of early and immature expertise. To reach their maximum capability, potential and put out the best services to the world, they must be of a certain level of maturity. And to get here, they need experience. Experience, however, is an argued topic. Some say that it is hard-earned, and some say that you can learn from that of from others. The collaboration with ANT Studios is designed to create an experience-gaining programme which fuses between the two. For this is just the beginning of our new project, we can not provide further information. Not yet. But we can guarantee one thing: it will be an experience that the world has never seen.
