Goal

The aim of the Accelerator Programme is simple: to help start-ups get in good-shape within the 3-month period, regardless of the stage their in. Every founder arrives with different stories: one might not have started working, and the other may have made considerable progress, but we coach both of those type of people and help them achieve similar progress within the same amount of time.

Process

During the programme, we support the taking-off of start-ups with two things: funding and coaching.
We invest $300,000 USD, equally, for all companies under two seperate safes:

  1. We invest $125,000 on a post-money safe, in return of 8% of your company ("$125,000 safe")
  2. We invest $175,000 on an uncapped safe with a Most Favored Nation (“MFN”) provision (the “MFN safe”)

Regarding coaching, start-ups are split into groups of three, and each group will be monitored by a Mentor, usually a 15+ years experience Senior Partner at Venturebox Capital or partnered firms.

Outcome

The outcome of your company’s hardwork, indefatigability, talent, depth and commitment will be displayed publicly on a day called Demo Day. In this particular event, you, as the founder, must pitch your business model to Venturebox’s VCs and Limited Partners. If they see potential, money injections and investments will be offered.

Alumni

The Accelerator Programme doesn’t end after 3 months! Once a member, always a member. You will be able to join the Venturebox Alumni, and you will be invited to exclusive networking parties, founder’s retreats, help each other and even meet with Venturebox’s founding team.
