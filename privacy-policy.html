Privacy Policy


At Venturebox, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of the personal information of our users. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and share the personal information of our users, as well as the rights and choices you have in relation to your personal information.

What information do we collect?

We collect personal information that you provide to us when you use our website or interact with us, such as when you create an account, register for an event, or submit a contact form. This information may include your name, email address, phone number, and any other information that you choose to provide.

We may also collect certain technical information when you use our website, such as your IP address, browser type, and device information. We may use cookies and other tracking technologies to collect this information, which helps us to improve our website and understand how our users are interacting with it.

How do we use your personal information?

We use the personal information that we collect for various purposes, including to:

  • Provide the services and information that you request
  • Communicate with you about our services and events
  • Respond to your inquiries and requests
  • Analyze and improve our website and services
  • We may also use your personal information for marketing purposes, such as to send you newsletters or other updates about our company and services. If you do not wish to receive marketing communications from us, you can opt out at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions provided in the communication.

Do we share your personal information?

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your personal information with certain third parties in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist us in providing our services and operating our website, such as hosting providers and marketing agencies
  • With business partners or sponsors of events or other initiatives that we are involved in
  • As required by law, such as in response to a subpoena or court order

We take reasonable steps to ensure that these third parties protect the privacy and security of your personal information in accordance with this privacy policy.

Your rights and choices

You have certain rights in relation to your personal information, including the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You may also have the right to object to the processing of your personal information or to request that it be transferred to another party.

Changes to this privacy policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or to comply with legal requirements. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about our privacy practices.

To exercise these rights, or if you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy our privacy practices, please contact us.
